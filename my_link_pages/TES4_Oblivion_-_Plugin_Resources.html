Description:

Plugin Resources is a mod for The Elder Scrolls 4: Oblivion that provides a library of pre-created records for modders to use. This allows their mods to be mergeable into Wrye Bash's Bashed Patch, thus saving ESP slots for the end user.

Modders may use my wiki pages to claim blank records provided by Plugin Resources which can then be used for every new record in their mod. Details on how Plugin Resources may be used are found in the download links and discussion threads.
